FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Get answers to common questions about MOOV Sport Studio and our personalized
training approach.
What makes MOOV different from a regular gym?
MOOV offers evidence-based training led by certified physiotherapists, small group sizes, and personalized programs focused on health and safety.
Do I need training experience to start?
No experience is needed. Our trainers will guide you from your first session and adapt the program to your level.
How many people can join group or personal sessions?
Group classes are limited to 6 participants. Personal training zone accommodates up to 4 people at a time.
Do you offer sessions for people recovering from injuries or with posture problems?
Yes, our physiotherapists specialize in rehabilitation and corrective exercise for injuries and posture issues.
What membership options are available?
We offer monthly subscriptions and flexible packages for individual, group, and split training.
Can I freeze my membership?
Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 2 months per year. Contact us for details.
